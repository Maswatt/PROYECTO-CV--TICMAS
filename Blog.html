Piezas de poesia

A veces, solo se necesita un momento y una pieza escrita para distenderse...

Tripping

Talking to irl spiritual masters. Relaxing by a fire. Singing, dancing shamelessly. Getting in touch with your ancestors. Drawing/painting/writing. Avoiding digital screens. Abstaining from instant gratification. Enjoying time with great friends. Shadow work. Chakra work. Abstaining from lies and procrastination, upholding the golden rule, and practicing moderation in all things (including moderation itself). Listening to Tibetan singing bowls on youtube (shoutout Templesounds)..

Growing Magic

Growing mushrooms at home is a skill any psychonaut should master. By keeping things clean, not rushing and showing some patience (don't peek too much and disturb the cultures!) you can have the best quality mushrooms in the world. On demand. All the time.To grow anything is a privilege. But to cultivate a substance that can heal your mind and soul and transform your consciousness without adverse side effects is the mystery and magic of nature, and it deserves your appreciation.

La tiranìa de la paz

The robber baron's cruelty may sometimes sleep, his cupidity may at some point be satiated; but those who torment us for our own good will torment us without end, for they do so with the approval of their own conscience." - C.S. Lewis

Los dìas del metal

YLas criaturas humanas construyen sostenes suntuosos para sentarse lejos de la tierra. ¿Sabrán ellos que queriendo elevarse están cayendo? Si el hombre teme sentarse sobre la tierra, algo malo le ha pasado a su alma. Hermanos husihuilkes, no olviden que es honra sentarse en la tierra. Sentarse en círculos sobre la tierra, sentarse en círculos con un fuego ardiendo justo en el centro. ¿Quién cambiaría eso por un grotesco armazón donde el hombre deshace su apostura? Sentarse en la tierra es la serena felicidad, el único bien que debemos anhelar.
